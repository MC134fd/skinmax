★
Recommendation

Direction 2 (Data-Rich) + Stacked layout

Why this fits Skinmax best:

  • Metric-first — traffic-light chips deliver the "skin breakdown first" goal without opening the detail view
  • Unified pattern — metric chips on face cards and nutrition pills on food cards use the same visual grammar
  • Illustration space — 56px media area is large enough for meaningful artwork, small enough to not dominate
  • Density — 3-4 cards visible without scrolling on iPhone 15/16
  • Extensible — chip pattern reusable in ScanHistoryView, FoodLogView, notifications
  • Stacked > Grid — grid loses metric detail and food names get truncated; stacked fits the Home scroll flow

Direction 4 is a strong alternative if you plan to invest heavily in illustration assets. Direction 3 is great for marketing screenshots but too tall for daily use.
