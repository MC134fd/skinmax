My read on the shortlist

  • Top pick — 02 Fraunces ExtraBold (soft): feels modern, feminine, warm, sits beautifully next to the coral apple without competing with it. Huge in the skincare space right now.
  • Runner-up — 04 DM Serif Display: more graphic punch, reads as "confident brand" not "clinical app."
  • If you want Cal-AI-clean — 06 Space Grotesk Bold: closest to the reference screenshot, stays neutral so the apple carries the personality.
  • Avoid — 07 Unbounded: too "SaaS," doesn't match the warm palette.

Once you pick one, next step is: bundle the font's .ttf, register it in Info.plist / Typography.swift (e.g. .gbBrand), and swap the greeting row in HomeView.swift for the lockup. Apple icon stays as SwiftUI vector (same shapes we built for the mascot, just smaller + no face).
