Glowbite — App Icon Concepts

Six directions, all in the apple + sparkles family. Each is shown at full size, mid (Settings), and small (Spotlight).

01 · Classic

The Friendly Apple

The full brand-doc concept executed faithfully — happy face, blush cheeks, leaf, bite, sparkles, coral→peach gradient. Reads warm and approachable; tested formula that converts on the App Store.

Settings
Spotlight

Best for: Mass-market Gen Z appeal. Most likely to make someone tap "install."

02 · Glow Aura

The Glowing One

Apple radiating an aura with rays + sparkles — leans hard into "glow." Eyes are blissful arcs (closed). Background has a soft radial gradient that feels like sunshine. Most differentiated from typical food apps.

Settings
Spotlight

Best for: Owning "glow" as a category word. Most poetic and aspirational.

03 · Wink

The Cheeky Bestie

Apple winking with tongue out. Maximum personality. Reads as "your gossip-y best friend who happens to know skincare." Will have the strongest emotional reaction in screenshots and TikToks.

Settings
Spotlight

Best for: Standing out on a crowded home screen. High-personality TikTok bait.

04 · Bold Minimal

The Aesthetic One

Cream apple silhouette on solid coral with one bold sparkle. No face. The most "Apple-design-language" / "iOS native aesthetic" of the bunch. Looks premium next to system apps.

Settings
Spotlight

Best for: Users who curate aesthetic home screens. Trades warmth for sophistication.

05 · Score Ring

Apple in the Glow Ring

Fuses the two strongest brand assets — the apple and the signature Glow Score ring. Apple sits inside an 85%-filled coral progress ring on a cream background. Most strategic: every time you see the icon you see the product's core mechanic.

Settings
Spotlight

Best for: Telling the product story in one glance. May read busy at very small sizes.

06 · Sparkle Burst

The Cosmic Apple

Apple with a starburst behind it — eyes are sparkles instead of dots. Coral background gradient, halo of light. Most "main character energy" — feels celebratory and rare. Great for launch hype.

Settings
Spotlight

Best for: Launch / hero marketing icon. Could pair with #04 as a seasonal alternate.

Tip: tap each icon to view full size. iOS rounds the corners automatically — your icon should be a 1024×1024 square; the system applies the squircle mask.
