The 5 motifs, every screen inherits them

⌞⌟
Scanner brackets

L-shaped marks frame the "moment of measurement" — Glow Score, scan previews, achievements.

😊
Character scores

The ring always has a face (🌟/😊/💫 by tier). No plain numbers without personality.

✨
Sparkles

Pairs/triplets only. Float around high scores, good food, streak milestones.

⚪
Soft highlights

Every coral surface gets a white ellipse top-left. Makes flat color feel hand-painted.

🍑
Peach gradients

No flat white screens. Every ScrollView goes cream→peach top to bottom.
